Estimated Time of Arrival

Purpose

This is a simple application that determines the driving time to get from the host to the guest. The host is the first person to enter a room and is typically the person driving to the guest. The guest is the second person to enter a room and is typically the person waiting for the host. This application is best used to give the guest an idea how long it will take the host to get to them. Keep in mind that the host's movement (or lack of), may change the estimated time to arrive.

How to use

  1. Enter an available room name.
  2. Send the url to your guest.
  3. View the estimated time to arrive.
Room name is required
