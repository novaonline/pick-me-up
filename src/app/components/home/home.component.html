Estimated Time of Arrival

How it works

If you want to let someone know how long it'll take you to get to them, enter a room name below. This is like a channel the two of you (and only you two) will tune into.

If you want to become a host, enter a room name that is available and you will become the host. It must be an empty room or else you might become a guest.

When you become a host, you'll notice a share feature that allows you to share the url to your guest. After that, you'll see the Estmated Time of Arrival
